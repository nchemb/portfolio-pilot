When you **track 401k, IRA, and taxable accounts together**, you gain a comprehensive view of your assets. This unified approach allows you to:

Aggregating your retirement accounts simplifies management. Imagine having all your data in one place—no more toggling between tabs or squinting at spreadsheets.

## How to Aggregate Your Retirement Accounts

### Step 1: Gather Your Account Information

Start by collecting all necessary data from each of your accounts:

1. **401k**: Check your current balance and investment allocations.
2. **IRA**: Note your contributions, earnings, and any fees.
3. **Taxable Accounts**: Record your holdings and recent transactions.
4. **ISA/SIPP**: Ensure you have updated statements, including any dividends reinvested.
